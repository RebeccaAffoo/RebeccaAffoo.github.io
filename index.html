My name is Rebecca Affoo, and I am a PhD student working under the supervision of Dr. Ruth Martin in the graduate program of Health and Rehabilitation Sciences at Western University, in the field of Speech and Language Sciences. I hold a clinical degree in the area of speech-language pathology, and I practiced clinically from 2008 to 2011 in a primary care facility, providing swallowing assessment and management for acutely ill older adults with dementia. Despite an overwhelming number of patients with concurrent cognitive and swallowing impairment, there is little evidence on this topic, and this imbalance motivated me to begin my doctoral studies and has influenced my research interests.
